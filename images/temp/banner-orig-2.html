Bakersfield, CA Indian Wedding by Avenue Blu3

March 11, 2014 Posted by: Shawna Comments

Gorgeous, gorgeous! This Maharani is bringing a ton of glam to her Indian wedding, from her unstoppable ruby lengha to her shimmering tikka to her intricate mehndi designs. Her makeup is truly flawless; pairing a bold red lip with soft gold shadow is always a fab idea! These portraits by Avenue Blu3 are a complete dream and I don't think a single style detail has been overlooked. While this bride counts the minutes until she meets her Raja at the gurdwara, I'll be browsing my way through the gallery!
